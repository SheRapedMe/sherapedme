My Landlord, Shelley Palmer, Raped & Abused Me for 10 Months, Then Her Dog Bit My Nose Off

For the second time in a year, I am homeless in Richmond, Virginia, surviving on SNAP assistance eaten in the grocery store and finding occasional shelter on Tinder when the weather turns foul. This is not my first time escaping abuse - it’s come from parents, partners, landlords, and doctors all my life. The last time it was this bad was in 2018 when my dog, dignity, and all my belongings were stolen as revenge for speaking out. This year, I’ve lost my nose, my final ounce of trust for women, and all my belongings again.

RVA's Homeless Connection Line intake number is a voicemail box I’ve been calling since November 2022, among many other organizations: VCU’s EmpowerNET, Daily Planet, Catholic Charities, RBHA, CARITAS, Central Virginia Legal Aid, Safe Harbor, VA Abuse Action Alliance, YWCA, Bon Secours. They’ve all been mostly useless, just passing me off to the next, or worse, are complicit in some of the abuse and trauma. They tell me I was raped and human trafficked but then ghost me when I ask for help reporting it.

After multiple public meetings and a pinky promise we wouldn’t have sex, I visited Landlord’s home but didn't spend the night, explaining I have narcolepsy, have been taken advantage of due to waking sleep-paralysis, and am thus self-conscious about sleeping around new acquaintances.

Eventually, Landlord convinced me to sleep over,, suggesting we wear onesies as a gesture of her promise and mentioning how unsafe being outside was. When I arrived that September evening, she provided me with an outfit that exposed my penis through a hole in the front while Landlord’s had a removable covering in the rear. When I questioned her motives, she said, “a promise is a promise” and “what’s the alternative?”

We both knew it was far too late and ugly outside for me to come up with secondary sleeping arrangements this far out into the suburbs. While we ate and watched TV, I stalled and limited what kissing and touching Landlord initiated that I could. The next day I had few memories of getting into bed, showed clear signs of PIV intercourse upon my body, and left Landlord’s home as quickly as I could.

Later, I received jokes about the onesies and sex from Landlord and her friends as if the promise had never existed. I was also told nude photos of my body and close-up photos of my erect penis were shared with Landlord’s friends and coworkers, people I had never met, in a manner that suggested I should feel proud.

As a last resort, I checked myself into the hospital emergency room where I lied about not wanting to live in order to get food, shelter, and meds. Inside HCA, I told doctors and social workers there about what some people on Tinder had done to me; nurses made sexual comments about the contents of my bag and while watching me change. I was discharged a week later with no safety plan, only another phone number that had never heard of me when I called, and like all others, had no beds.

That same night, Landlord invited me to stay permanently, again saying things like “what’s the alternative?”. Over the next 10 months, this would become her catchphrase for instilling fear and helplessness, especially when explaining how I would be homeless and starving otherwise. In 3 months of calls all I had managed was 3 nights in a crack motel billed to my Medicaid, so without another option, I said yes. I still managed to resist moving in all my belongings, computers, and keepsakes all, for another month, mainly because Landlord’s dog was dangerous and destroyed everything.

One evening before the holidays, Landlord came home from work and quickly downed over a dozen shots of tequila then yanked my clothes down while I was cooking and shoved my penis in her mouth, causing the worst cataplexy episode I’ve had in recent memory. Landlord's drug abuse was frequent and extended to prescription opioids and muscle relaxers. She even bullied an ex-FWB into giving her pills that Landlord admits almost put her in the hospital psych ward.

After that incident, I realized survival was my only goal and resolved to put on a facade for my own safety, "consenting" only to what I thought or was told would keep a roof over my head. I focused on my mind’s health, plugging into Richmond Behavioral Health Authority, where I reported some of Landlord’s abuse and received jokes in return from my assigned psychiatric nurse. RBHA then cut me off from my meds when I reported this to the VDHP.

The rapes,especially while I was asleep, abuse, and forms of control from Landlord continued and are too numerous to list. I got really good at faking orgasms.

My disability application has been pending medical review for almost 300 days (countless on-foot vehicle collisions, two GSWs and a stabbing, narcolepsy with cataplexy, arthritis and asthma, as well as ADHD, cPTSD, et al). Big home projects like plumbing, building a patio, etc were also my responsibility despite these disabilities.

Any work I was allowed to do was either for her or her friends.
